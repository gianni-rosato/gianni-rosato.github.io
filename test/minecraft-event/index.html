Minecraft Weapons 2024

Welcome back, it has certainly been a while!

General Server Information

1.21

weapons.gilfoyle.net

Thursday night: Jul 11, 2024 @ 7pm EST

There are six special items strewn about the server guarded in structures that protect them with mobs, traps, etc.

Your goal as a team is to get one member of your team to wield all of the special items at once; then, your team wins the game.

There are four teams (so far):

  • Red
  • Green
  • Blue
  • Purple

You will be assigned a team color once you join the server with your teammates.

There is a modpack required to play. You can download it below.

Modpack

The modpack includes proximity chat via Simple Voice Chat. You can use this to communicate with your teammates as well as other teams; there is no central Discord server or forum for this event, so physically encountering other teams is the only way to communicate with them.

Team Rules

  • Friendly fire is disabled.
  • You can't switch teams once you are on a particular team.
  • You can only see your teammates' nametags.
  • You cannot push your teammates (disabled collisions).
  • You have to be loyal to your team; you cannot play for another team.

Alliances are allowed, but they are based on trust alone. Anyone can betray anyone else if they're on different teams, and I won't enforce alliances.

World Information

The world has a world border with a 2560-block (160 chunk) radius.

The Nether is disabled.

Some structures have special items, some don't. I've tried to make this obvious, but clues will help you know for sure.

You can pay for me to give your team clues regarding locations of the special items. Prices are listed below.
