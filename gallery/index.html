Why?

I believe that at its best, effective multimedia compression is a way to allow for artists to express themselves in novel ways to massive audiences. I take photos sometimes, and I wouldn't consider myself a photographer, but I'd like to share some of my pictures with you here. I hope you enjoy.

Please note that this page loads ~20 MB (AVIF) or ~40 MB (High-fidelity JPEG XL) of data if you scroll through all of the photos. This page is best experienced with a browser that supports JPEG XL.
